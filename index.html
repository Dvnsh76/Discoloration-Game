Welcome to the Discoloration Game!

Instructions

1. Click on a cell to toggle its state (green or grey).

2. Clicking a cell will also toggle its neighboring cells.

3. The goal is to turn all cells green.

4. Use the "Restart Level" button to reset the current level.

5. Use the "Solve Level" button to get the answer.

Good luck!
